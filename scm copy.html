Budget-Friendly Travel Planning

How much money will you need for your trip to Norway? You should plan to spend around kr1,283 ($120) per day on your vacation in Norway, which is the average daily price based on the expenses of other visitors. Past travelers have spent, on average, kr347 ($32) on meals for one day and kr203 ($19) on local transportation. Also, the average hotel price in Norway for a couple is kr1,320 ($123). So, a trip to Norway for two people for one week costs on average kr17,956 ($1,676). All of these average travel prices have been collected from other travelers to help you plan your own travel budget.

Independent Travel

The average price paid for one person for accommodation in Norway is kr660. For two people sharing a typical double-occupancy hotel room, the average price paid for a hotel room in Norway is kr1,320. This cost is from the reported spending of actual travelers.

Average Daily Costs

While meal prices in Norway can vary, the average cost of food in Norway is kr347 per day. Based on the spending habits of previous travelers, when dining out an average meal in Norway should cost around kr139 per person. Breakfast prices are usually a little cheaper than lunch or dinner. The price of food in sit-down restaurants in Norway is often higher than fast food prices or street food prices.

Entertainment and activities in Norway typically cost an average of kr184 per person, per day based on the spending of previous travelers. This includes fees paid for admission tickets to museums and attractions, day tours, and other sightseeing expenses.

The average person spends about kr94 on alcoholic beverages in Norway per day. The more you spend on alcohol, the more fun you might be having despite your higher budget.

On average, people spend kr45 on bottled water in Norway per day. The public water in Norway is considered safe to drink.
