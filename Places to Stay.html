Hostels are not all that plentiful across Norway (they’re usually just in the larger cities) so be sure to plan accordingly. Here are my suggested places to stay while you’re in Norway:

ANKER HOSTEL

Located in the fantastic district of Grünerløkka and less than 15 minutes' walk from Oslo's busy main street Karl Johan, Anker Hostel gives you an unique experience from different areas in Oslo. Grünerløkka has risen to become Oslo`s most trendy area, with shops, a variety of restaurants and fantastic bars surrounding beautiful parks and other attractions. In addition to this, concert venues such as Oslo Spectrum, Sentrum Scene and Rockefeller are only a few minutes walk away.

Anker Hostel offers spacious rooms and accommodation suitable for all age groups in a relaxed and international atmosphere. All rooms are equipped with kitchen and bathroom. Free Wi-Fi access is available in all rooms and in our fantastic public areas, including library (Mezzanine), lobby, lounge area and bar.

One set of bedlinen/towels are included in the price. Additional sets can be rented in the reception for NOK 70.

Parking (NOK 380) - per day

Please note that Anker hostel is a cashless hostel.

COCHS PENSJONAT

Cochs is a good and popular pension, centrally located right behind the Royal Palace.

Cochs offers a friendly atmosphere with clean and tidy rooms. The 88 rooms are offered in three categories: Best rooms, Standard Rooms and Value Rooms.

Some of the Best Rooms and Standard Rooms have a kitchenette, others have a boiler and fridge. The Value Rooms have a bathroom in the corridor and no cooking facilities.

All rooms are non-smoking.

Breakfast is not included. However, most rooms have a small tea kitchen, and guests can buy breakfast packages at a special price at Kaffebrenneriet on the ground floor.

Please note :
Cancellation policy : 18.00 the day before arrival.
Check in : 14.00.
Check out : 11.00.
Credit cards accepted (except AMEX).
Breakfast not included.
Taxes included.

HI BERGEN HOSTEL MONTANA

Welcome to HI Bergen Hostel Montana, the coolest place to stay during your trip to Bergen!

We're located up on the hillside of Mount Ulriken, about 3.6 km from Bergen City Centre, and super easy to reach by public transportation. Plus, there's a grocery store right next door and many different hiking trails just around the corner. Did we mention free parking? Yeah, we got that too, exclusive for our lovely guests!

We welcome backpackers, groups, and families to our international community of travelers all year round. And we've got options for everyone - whether you're looking for a private room, be it single, twin, triple, or quadruple (and more!), or prefer a shared dormitory for making new friends and sharing travel stories while saving some bucks!

Now, let's talk about the good stuff - food! We offer a complete all-you-can-eat breakfast buffet to keep you fueled up for all your adventures FOR FREE. And not to brag, it is amazing.

No worries if you left your bed linens and towels behind, we've got your back! You can rent a set from us at a super affordable rate of just 70 NOK for the whole duration of your stay, or if you prefer, bring your own for absolutely no charge. Just a friendly reminder, bed linens are an absolute must, and sleeping bags are not allowed. Consider yourself duly informed, my friend!

We've got Wi-Fi so you can keep up with all your social media posts (#bergenhostelmontana), laundry facilities to keep your clothes fresh and clean, a small gym, and two guest kitchens equipped with everything you need to show off your chef skills. And when you're ready to relax, we've got plenty of options too: chill out in our TV lounge or challenge your new hostel friends to a game of foosball or table tennis in the games room.

Our international volunteers are always planning fun activities for our guests - from hikes and BBQs to music sessions. And let's not forget the free Coffee and Cookies activity, every evening in the common room (because we all know that's the most important meal of the day).

Our hostel is HI Quality and Sustainability certified, so you can rest easy knowing you're staying somewhere that cares about the environment and a greener planet!

And last but not least, our outdoor areas are perfect for taking in the breathtaking views of the mountains. And there are even BBQ facilities so you can grill up some delicious food while you're at it.

Bergen Hostel Montana also has its own restaurant with a varied and delicious menu prepared by the hostel's chef: sandwiches, pizzas, pasta, and Norwegian traditional food. Open every day from 16:00 to 22:00 (subject to change).

So, pack your bags, put on your hiking boots, and come join us at HI Bergen Hostel Montana for the adventure of a lifetime!

LILLEHAMMER HOSTEL

Lillehammer Hostel is located AT the bus and train station in the city centre of Lillehammer. The hostel is actually in the train station building! Guests could not have a better starting point for seeing all that Lillehammer has to offer, including shopping, attractions, restaurants and hiking. The shopping street in Lillehammer is only 100m walking distance from the hostel.

All rooms are equipped with an ensuite shower/toilet and free Wi-Fi. Bed linen and towels are included in the price and beds are made before you arrive. There is a fully equipped guest kitchen, payment parking and cafe/restaurant. Breakfast is included.

Most of the sights like the open air museum Maihaugen, Lillehammer Art Museum, Sigrid Undsets home Bjerkebaek, the Olympic Museum and the Olympic Park are within walking distance. There are buses to the family park Hunderfossen and Lilleputthammer. Hafjell Alpine centre is the closest ski centre to the Lillehammer Hostel.

TROMSO ACTIVITIES HOSTEL

The hostel is mainly focused on providing budget accommodation and experiences for young travellers. It is an excellent place to meet other travellers and there is always something happening. We run budged Northern lights tours, and other activities throughout the year.

Tromsø Activities Hostel is set in the Historic city center of Tromsø. Close to all the main sights of Tromsø. Polar museum, Arctic Cathedral, Fjellheisen (Cable car) and right in the city center, we provide a perfect base to explore the area.

Our hostel is a mix of new and old, with home built beds and furniture. Comfortable and wide bunk beds. 24 hours automatic door lock for the front doors. 4 Simple kitchens for basic cooking. The two buildings are themed in colors reflecting the Arctic sky, with one building colored in the colors of the Northern lights, and the other themed for the never ending sunset of summer.
